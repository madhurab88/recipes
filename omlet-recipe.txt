Ingredients:
 - 2 eggs
 - onions finely chopped
 - salt
 - pepper

Directions:
 1. Heat the pan on the stove and add some oil.
 
 2. once the oil is moderately hot, add onions and salute it.
 
 3. Add the eggs to the pan and stir it white it cooks on the pan.

 4. add some salt and pepper to taste.
